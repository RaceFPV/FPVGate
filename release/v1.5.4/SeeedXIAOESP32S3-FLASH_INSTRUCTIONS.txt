FPVGate v1.5.4 - Flash Instructions (Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32S3, 8MB)

Using esptool.py:
1) Install: pip install esptool
2) Connect the board via USB
3) Run:
   esptool.py --chip esp32s3 --port COMx --baud 460800 write_flash -z \
     0x0000   SeeedXIAO-ESP32S3-bootloader.bin \
     0x8000   SeeedXIAO-ESP32S3-partitions.bin \
     0x10000  SeeedXIAO-ESP32S3-firmware.bin \
     0x410000 SeeedXIAO-ESP32S3-littlefs.bin

Notes:
- Replace COMx with your serial port.
- LittleFS offset for 8MB is 0x410000.
- After flashing, the device will reboot automatically.
