FPVGate v1.5.4 - Flash Instructions (ESP32-S3 Super Mini, 4MB)

Using esptool.py:
1) Install: pip install esptool
2) Connect the board via USB
3) Run:
   esptool.py --chip esp32s3 --port COMx --baud 460800 write_flash -z \
     0x0000   ESP32S3-SuperMini-4MB-bootloader.bin \
     0x8000   ESP32S3-SuperMini-4MB-partitions.bin \
     0x10000  ESP32S3-SuperMini-4MB-firmware.bin \
     0x290000 ESP32S3-SuperMini-4MB-littlefs.bin

Notes:
- Replace COMx with your serial port.
- LittleFS offset for 4MB Super Mini is 0x290000.
- After flashing, the device will reboot automatically.
